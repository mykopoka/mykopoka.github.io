A quick guide to start using Kopoka

This guide is made to help you start using Kopoka.

1) Overview

Kopoka's basic feature is mobile attendance. Here are the steps to start using Kopoka's mobile attendance:

  1. Register your organization at Sign Up page
  2. Wait for your site to be activated by Kopoka, then after you received activation notification mail, login to Kopoka web app and register staffs
  3. Tell your staffs to install Kopoka mobile app from Google Play
  4. Staffs will need to login to Kopoka via the app, using your Organization ID, registered mail address and password.
  5. Staffs can then start using Kopoka mobile app to record attendance.

Each step above is explained in detail below.

2) How to login to Kopoka web app staffs

Here is how to login to Kopoka web app

  1. Open Kopoka webb app's main login page, enter your Organization ID and click "Login".
  2. You will be brought to your organization's login page. Enter your email address and password to login. You can bookmark this page to directly go to your organization's login page next time.

3) How to register staffs

Here is how to register staffs at Kopoka. You need to be accessing Kopoka web app as HR Administrator (i.e., your role needs to be "HR Admin") in order to add staffs. Look at upper right part of the screen where it says "Current role:". If it is currently "Staff", change it to "HR Admin" by going to Settings screen, as follows:

  1. Login to Kopoka web app if you are not logged in.
  2. At upper left part of the screen, click on your name or job title, then click on "Accounts"
  3. At "Switch Role" section, click on "Active Role" drop down list and select "HR Admin".

After making sure that your current role that is shown at upper right part of the screen is "HR Admin", add your staffs as follows:

  1. Click on "People" at the menu on the left, then click on "Staff".
  2. Click on "New Staff" button, fill out the entries and click "Save".

Compulsory fields are as follows:

"Main Information" tab: First Name, Date of Birth, Gender
"Organization and Job Information" tab: Employee ID, Job Title, Employment Type
"Contact Information" tab: Email
"Payroll" tab: Marital Status

4) How to install Kopoka mobile app

Go to Google Play and install Kopoka mobile app.

5) How to use mobile attendance

Start the app, then login with provided Organization ID, registered mail address and password. After logging in, you will be presented with main screen. Tap on "Time Card". Here is where you clock in and clock out by tapping at appropriate buttons.

  1. When start working from your workplace, tap on "IN" to clock in.
  2. When you start working outside of your workplace, then tap on "REMOTE IN".
  3. When finished working at your workplace, tap on "OUT" to clock in.
  4. When you finished working outside of your workplace, then tap on "REMOTE OUT".

Anytime during working hours, you can tap "START BREAK" to mark the beginning of break time, and then tap on "STOP BREAK" to mark the end of your break time.

6) How to see and download attendance data

Attendance data can be viewed by HR Administrator currently on Kopoka web app. First login to Kopoka web app as stated in "How to login to Kopoka web app staffs" at no.2 above. Then:

  1. Click on "Personnel" menu, and click on "Attendance Summary". Attendance data for each staff for the particular month will be shown.
  2. Select Organization Unit, month, employee number or name, and then click "View" for limiting view to certain data.
  3. Click on "Download Report" to download the data as Excel file.
